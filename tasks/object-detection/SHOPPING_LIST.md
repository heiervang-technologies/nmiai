# Store Photography Priority List

**Purpose:** Products to photograph at Norwegian grocery stores to improve our object detection model.
Products are ranked by composite risk score (higher = more impact on our competition score).

## How to Use This List

1. Open this on your phone
2. Visit Meny or SPAR (Kiwi already covered in existing photos)
3. Follow the stops in order
4. Take LANDSCAPE photos, no flash, max resolution, 1-2m from shelf
5. Wide shots of full sections + close-ups of specific products

## Photography Tips Reminder

- **Orientation:** LANDSCAPE (horizontal)
- **Flash:** OFF (reflections ruin product labels)
- **Resolution:** Maximum your phone allows
- **Distance:** 1-2 meters from shelf
- **Angle:** Straight-on, perpendicular to shelf face
- **Coverage:** All shelf levels (top to bottom)
- **Price tags:** Keep visible when possible
- **Target:** 30-100 products visible per wide shot
- **Close-ups:** For confusable products (Wasa sandwich, crispbreads, tea boxes)

## Time Estimate

~35-45 photos across 8 stops, approximately 25-30 minutes in store.
